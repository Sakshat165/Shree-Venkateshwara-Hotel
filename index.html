Authentic Materials

Tamil South Indian cuisine is celebrated for its bold flavors, aromatic spices, and a wide array of ingredients that contribute to its rich and diverse dishes. Here at Shree Venkateshwara Hotel we use ingredients straight supplied from south to match the same flavour and that's our guarantee.In addition to that our motto is similar "Taste the Difference.."

Delivery

At Shree Venkateshwara Hotel, we understand that sometimes you crave the flavors of our authentic South Indian cuisine but may not have the opportunity to dine with us in person. That's why we are delighted to offer a food delivery service that brings the taste of our culinary excellence right to your doorstep.(Just for Keshav Nagar)

Online-Order

Through these popular food delivery platforms, we are able to reach a wide delivery radius, ensuring that our delicious food is accessible to customers in various neighborhoods.With Zomato and Swiggy, you can track the status of your order in real-time. Know exactly when your meal is being prepared, when it's out for delivery, and when it will arrive at your doorstep.

About Us

Welcome to Shree Venkateshwara Hotel

Shree Venkateshwara Hotel, a venerable establishment in the heart of Pune serving authentic South Indian delicacies. From 2021, we have been a cherished destination for locals and travelers, offering a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Whether you're a food enthusiast, a traveler seeking an authentic experience, or a local looking for a memorable dining destination, Shree Venkateshwara Hotel welcomes you to embark on a journey of taste, tradition, and togetherness. We invite you to savor the flavors, make lasting memories, and be a part of our story.

Shree Venkateshwara Hotel is not just a place to dine, it's an opportunity to feel the taste of South. Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We value your feedback and continuously strive to improve our offerings to exceed your expectations. In response to the changing times and the growing demand for convenience, Shree Venkateshwara Hotel offers online ordering and delivery services. You can now enjoy our delicious dishes in the comfort of your own home, making it easier than ever to savor our culinary creations. Our commitment to your happiness is unwavering, and we aim to make every visit to Shree Venkateshwara Hotel a memorable one.
